San Juan River

Open San Juan River on the map →
  • Watershed area: about 66,987 ha.
  • Old growth still standing: about 6,137 ha of 250-plus-year forest (VRI), including about 5,697 ha mapped as old growth by the province's panel (TAP) and 3,320 ha of legal Old Growth Management Area.
  • Old growth flagged for deferral: about 2,296 ha.
  • Recorded logging: about 11,422 ha (17.1 percent of the watershed); about 13,261 ha is now young regrowth under 60 years.
  • Roads and water: about 1,211 km of forest road, 1,396 km of stream, and an estimated 622 road-stream crossings (a GIS estimate, not a field count).
  • Recent high-risk blocks (harvest 2015 or later, near old growth, deferral, water, fish, or recreation): 44. Highest-impact: OPENING_ID 1770235 (harvest 2020, priority-deferral; old growth (TAP); stream); OPENING_ID 1770573 (harvest 2022, old growth (TAP); stream); OPENING_ID 1725472 (harvest 2019, legal OGMA; stream; fish record).
  • The ask: before more old growth in San Juan River is approved, sold, or cut, the province should disclose the full-cost accounting and the site-level records. Ready-to-file requests for the named blocks are in the records-request guide.
