Logging proposed near salmon waters

Salmon and trout need cold, clean, stable streams, and logging in the slopes above a stream can change its water and its spawning gravel. Across the four areas, 77 blocks are proposed or approved for logging within 300 m of a mapped fish record. 5 still have an open comment period.
