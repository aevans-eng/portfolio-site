These are the 71 blocks proposed or approved for logging around Gold River on the province's Forest Operations Map, ranked by the public value they sit closest to. 61 overlap mapped old growth or a deferral area, or sit near a fish record or recreation site. None are open for public comment right now; the most recent comment period closed 2026-04-02. You can still file a records request and watch for the next FOM posting.

This is forward-looking: what is coming, not past clearcuts. Overlap and proximity are reasons to look and to comment, not proof of harm. Each block's comment period is set by its Forest Operations Map project; once that window closes you can still file a records request.
