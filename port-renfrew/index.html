Public-interest data project · Port Renfrew, Vancouver Island

What the public can see before old forest is cut.

Built entirely from official public data, not leaks or allegations. It shows what the records reveal about logging around Port Renfrew, what is still hidden, and why the public should see the full cost before old-growth or high-risk forest is approved, sold, or cut.

No corruption, illegality, or harm is claimed. Overlaps and screening numbers are reasons to ask for records, not proof of harm.
