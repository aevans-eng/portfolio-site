Lens Creek

  • Watershed area: about 12,285 ha.
  • Old growth still standing: about 344 ha of 250-plus-year forest (VRI), including about 258 ha mapped as old growth by the province's panel (TAP) and 248 ha of legal Old Growth Management Area.
  • Old growth flagged for deferral: about 73 ha.
  • Recorded logging: about 2,356 ha (19.2 percent of the watershed); about 997 ha is now young regrowth under 60 years.
  • Roads and water: about 126 km of forest road, 265 km of stream, and an estimated 39 road-stream crossings (a GIS estimate, not a field count).
  • Recent high-risk blocks in the register: none labelled to this watershed (some nearby blocks may be recorded under the broader South Island area).
  • The ask: before more old growth in Lens Creek is approved, sold, or cut, the province should disclose the full-cost accounting and the site-level records. Ready-to-file requests for the named blocks are in the records-request guide.
