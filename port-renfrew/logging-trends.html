How much is being logged

Across Port Renfrew, Lake Cowichan, Nitinat, Alberni Valley, Sooke, Campbell River, and Gold River, public records show about 70,724 hectares logged in the last ten years and 265,436 hectares on record in total. Another 10,966 hectares are proposed or approved to come. Each bar is one year of recorded harvest.
