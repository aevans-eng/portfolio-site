How much is being logged

Across Port Renfrew, Lake Cowichan, and Nitinat, public records show about 34,955 hectares logged in the last ten years and 99,552 hectares on record in total. Another 2,922 hectares are proposed or approved to come. Each bar is one year of recorded harvest.
