Get the data

Everything on this site is built from the BC government's open data. The tables and map layers below are the processed versions, free for anyone to download and reuse: journalists, researchers, community groups, or anyone checking the work. The CSVs are the per-block screening tables; the GeoJSON files are the map layers.

Please attribute the original data to the Province of British Columbia (Open Government Licence - British Columbia) and link back here for the processing. Every figure traces to its official source on the sources page.
